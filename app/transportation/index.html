CMU transportation information

    For the visitor, The CMU (Chiang Mai University) is a recommended place that you must NOT miss But some of you may confuse about CMU transportation. Which one is for Visitor or which one is for a student. The CMU transportation has TWO type of it. The first one is a purple bus that for the students. And the other is a green bus that is for visitors.
    The Green Bus is a bus that for the visitor who wants to travel at Chiang Mai University (CMU). The service charge for an adult is 60 bath, and children are 20 baths. The start point and end point is at the front gate of the university. And DON’T be worry we have many languages of explanation in the bus. For example English and Chinese.
    The route for the visitor bus starts from the front gate and move through the faculty of political and public administration, through the traffic intersection (Have only one traffic intersection) and move to a clock tower around about, the one CMU landmark. And then go on the football field, girl’s dormitory, central library, and the famous landmark “Ang-kaew.”
    The Green bus will stop only one place which is “Ang-kaew” around 15 minutes for the visitor to keep an impression and take a photo with our most famous landmark.
    The Purple Bus is a bus that for the student and staff of Chiang Mai University who wants to transport in the university in free charge. Open at 7.00-22.00 every day in 6 routes that cover around the entire university and many buses that ready to service.
    1.Route green: start from Chiang Mai university service center to Rugby field.
    2.Route orange: start from Fai Hin market to Learning center.
    3.Route red: start from in front of girl dormitory 3 to Student Affairs building.
    4.Route blue: start from in front of girl dormitory 3 to corner between dormitory boy 3,4.
    5.Route purple: start from in front of girl dormitory 3 to bus stop in front of girl dormitory 2
    6.Route navy blue: start from Chiang Mai university service center to pavilion buddha.
